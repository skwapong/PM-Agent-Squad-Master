# KNOWLEDGE BASE 9A: TIKTOK ADVERTISING BEST PRACTICES (PART 1 OF 2)

## PLATFORM OVERVIEW
TikTok is the fastest-growing social media platform with over 1.5 billion monthly active users globally. The platform is video-first, entertainment-focused, and algorithm-driven with exceptional organic reach potential. TikTok's advertising platform offers sophisticated targeting, creative tools, and unique engagement opportunities. Average time spent: 95 minutes per day per user.

## TIKTOK USER DEMOGRAPHICS & BEHAVIOR
**Key Demographics:**
- 60% of users aged 16-34 (Gen Z and younger Millennials)
- Slightly female-skewed (57% female, 43% male)
- Global platform with strong presence in US, Europe, Southeast Asia
- High engagement rates (average 5.3% vs. 0.6% on Instagram)
- 90% of users access TikTok multiple times per day

**User Behavior:**
- Entertainment and discovery-focused (not connecting with friends)
- Full-screen, sound-on video consumption
- Rapid content consumption (average 15-60 second videos)
- Highly engaged with trends, challenges, and viral content
- 67% of users say TikTok inspired them to shop
- Strong community interaction through duets, stitches, comments
- Algorithm-driven "For You Page" (FYP) creates high discoverability

**Content Categories:**
- Entertainment, comedy, dance, music, beauty, fashion, food, DIY, education, life hacks, behind-the-scenes, product reviews

## TIKTOK CAMPAIGN OBJECTIVES

### Awareness Objectives
**Reach:**
- Maximize number of people who see ads
- Best for: Brand awareness, new product launches, event promotion
- Optimization: Impressions, reach
- Pricing: CPM (cost per 1,000 impressions)

**Video Views:**
- Maximize video view volume
- View counted: 2 seconds or more (standard), 6 seconds (6s view optimization)
- Best for: Content promotion, brand storytelling, engagement building
- Optimization: 2-second views, 6-second views
- Pricing: CPV (cost per view) or CPM

### Consideration Objectives
**Traffic:**
- Drive clicks to website or app
- Best for: Driving traffic to landing pages, blog content, product pages
- Optimization: Clicks, landing page views
- Pricing: CPC (cost per click)

**Community Interaction:**
- Drive engagement with TikTok profile
- Best for: Building TikTok following, increasing profile visits
- Optimization: Profile visits, follower increases
- Pricing: CPM

**Video Views (Consideration):**
- Focused on longer, quality views
- 6-second view optimization available
- Best for: Product education, tutorials, consideration content
- Optimization: 6-second views, completed views

### Conversion Objectives
**Lead Generation:**
- Collect leads via Instant Forms (on-platform forms)
- Best for: B2B lead gen, newsletter signups, quote requests
- Optimization: Form submissions
- Pricing: CPM or cost per lead

**Website Conversions:**
- Drive specific actions on website
- Requires TikTok Pixel installation
- Best for: E-commerce purchases, sign-ups, registrations
- Optimization: Conversions, value-based optimization
- Pricing: oCPM (optimized CPM), CPA targeting available

**App Installs:**
- Drive mobile app downloads
- Deep linking to specific app pages
- Best for: Mobile apps, games, subscription apps
- Optimization: App installs, in-app events
- Pricing: CPM, CPI (cost per install) bidding

**Product Sales (TikTok Shop):**
- Drive direct sales through TikTok Shop
- Integrated e-commerce experience
- Best for: Brands with TikTok Shop catalog
- Optimization: Product purchases
- Pricing: Value-based bidding

## TIKTOK AUDIENCE TARGETING

### Demographic Targeting
**Age:** 13-17, 18-24, 25-34, 35-44, 45-54, 55+
**Gender:** All, Male, Female
**Location:** Countries, states/provinces, cities, zip codes, DMA
- Radius targeting available (1-50 mile radius)
**Languages:** 75+ languages supported
**Devices:** iOS, Android, specific device models, OS versions, connection type (WiFi, cellular)

### Interest & Behavior Targeting
**Interest Categories:**
- 200+ interest categories across verticals
- Broad categories: Beauty & Personal Care, Business & Industry, Education, Family & Relationships, Fashion & Apparel, Financial Services, Food & Beverage, Health & Fitness, Hobbies & Interests, Home & Garden, News & Entertainment, Pets, Shopping, Sports & Outdoors, Technology, Travel, Vehicles

**Behavior Targeting:**
- Video interactions (liked, commented, shared specific content types)
- Creator interactions (followed specific creators or content styles)
- Hashtag interactions
- Shopping behaviors

**Engagement-Based:**
- Users who engaged with your TikTok account
- Video viewers (75%, 95% completion)
- Ad engagers (clicked, liked, shared ads)

### Custom Audiences
**Customer File:**
- Upload customer emails, phone numbers, advertiser IDs
- Match rate typically 30-60%
- Minimum 1,000 users recommended

**Website Traffic:**
- TikTok Pixel-based retargeting
- Target based on pages visited, events completed
- Time windows: 1-180 days
- Best for warm audiences familiar with brand

**App Activity:**
- Users who took actions in mobile app
- Requires TikTok SDK integration
- Target by specific in-app events

**Engagement Audiences:**
- Video viewers (2s, 6s, 25%, 50%, 75%, 95%, 100%)
- Profile viewers
- Ad engagers
- TikTok Shop interactions
- Time windows: 7, 15, 30, 60, 90, 180, 365 days

**Lead Generation:**
- People who opened or submitted Instant Forms
- Time windows: 30, 60, 90, 180, 365 days

### Lookalike Audiences
- Create audiences similar to existing customers or engagers
- Similarity range: Narrow, Balanced, Broad
- Source audience minimum: 100 users (1,000+ recommended)
- Best practices:
  - Use high-quality source (purchasers, high-value customers)
  - Start with Narrow for precision
  - Test Balanced for scale
  - Use Broad when sufficient budget and conversion volume
- Can exclude source audience to reach new people only

### Automatic Targeting
- TikTok's algorithm finds best audiences
- Minimal targeting restrictions
- Requires strong conversion data and creative
- Best for accounts with 50+ conversions per week
- Leverages platform's powerful recommendation engine

## TIKTOK AD FORMATS

### In-Feed Ads
**Characteristics:**
- Appears in For You Page feed (native placement)
- Full-screen vertical video (9:16)
- Up to 60 seconds (9-15 seconds recommended)
- Includes interactive elements (like, comment, share, follow, CTA)
- Skippable after 3-5 seconds

**Specifications:**
- Video: 540 x 960 pixels minimum (720 x 1280 or 1080 x 1920 recommended)
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 (vertical), 1:1 (square), 16:9 (horizontal - not recommended)
- Video length: 5-60 seconds (9-15 seconds optimal)
- File size: Up to 500MB
- Format: .mp4, .mov, .mpeg, .avi, .webm

**Ad Copy:**
- Ad text: 100 characters (12-80 recommended for full visibility)
- Call-to-action options: Shop Now, Learn More, Sign Up, Download, Apply Now, Book Now, Contact Us

**Best for:**
- Most versatile format
- All campaign objectives
- Broad reach and engagement
- Testing creative concepts

### Spark Ads
**Characteristics:**
- Boost organic TikTok posts (yours or others with permission)
- Native, authentic appearance
- Retains original post engagement (likes, comments)
- Can use your own posts or creator posts
- Includes "Sponsored" label

**Advantages:**
- Higher authenticity and trust
- Leverage high-performing organic content
- Creator partnerships and user-generated content
- Retains social proof from organic engagement
- Often 20-30% better CTR than standard In-Feed

**How to Use:**
- Create organic post on TikTok account
- Toggle "Ad Authorization" in post settings
- Select authorized post in Ads Manager
- Add targeting and budget

**Best for:**
- User-generated content campaigns
- Influencer partnerships
- High-performing organic content amplification
- Building trust and authenticity

### TopView Ads
**Characteristics:**
- First thing users see when opening TikTok app
- Full-screen, sound-on video
- Up to 60 seconds
- Premium placement with 100% share of voice
- High impact and visibility

**Specifications:**
- Similar to In-Feed ads but premium placement
- Video length: 5-60 seconds
- Full-screen takeover on app open

**Pricing:**
- Premium pricing (CPM typically $10-20+)
- Often sold on reservation basis
- Minimum spend requirements vary by market

**Best for:**
- Major product launches
- Brand awareness campaigns
- High-budget, high-impact moments
- Seasonal/holiday campaigns
- Building immediate brand recall

### Brand Takeover
**Characteristics:**
- Static image or 3-5 second video
- Appears immediately on app open
- Exclusive: One advertiser per category per day
- Clickable to landing page or hashtag challenge

**Specifications:**
- Image: 1080 x 1920 pixels
- Video: 3-5 seconds, 1080 x 1920 pixels
- No sound for video

**Pricing:**
- Premium, reservation-based
- Category exclusivity
- High minimum spend

**Best for:**
- Maximum visibility and impact
- Product launches
- Major announcements
- Time-sensitive campaigns

### Branded Hashtag Challenge
**Characteristics:**
- Encourages user participation and content creation
- Custom hashtag and challenge page
- Discovery banner placement
- Can include custom effects and stickers
- Runs typically 3-6 days

**Components:**
- Challenge instructions and hashtag
- Banner placement on Discover page
- Sample videos demonstrating challenge
- Optional: Custom branded effects/filters

**Pricing:**
- Premium pricing ($150,000+ for full package)
- Includes guaranteed impressions and placements

**Best for:**
- Viral brand awareness
- User-generated content campaigns
- Community engagement
- Brand affinity building
- Entertainment and participation

**Success Factors:**
- Simple, fun, replicable challenge
- Engaging demonstration videos
- Trend alignment
- Clear instructions
- Incentive or motivation to participate

### Branded Effects
**Characteristics:**
- Custom 2D/3D effects, filters, stickers
- Users can apply to their own videos
- Appears in Effects gallery
- Typically 10-day placement

**Pricing:**
- Premium ($100,000+ typically)
- Development costs for custom effects

**Best for:**
- Immersive brand experiences
- Product visualization
- Beauty, fashion, entertainment brands
- Long-term user engagement
