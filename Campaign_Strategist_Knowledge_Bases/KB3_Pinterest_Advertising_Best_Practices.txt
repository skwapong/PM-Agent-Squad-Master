# KNOWLEDGE BASE 3: PINTEREST ADVERTISING BEST PRACTICES

## PLATFORM OVERVIEW
Pinterest is a visual discovery platform with 450+ million monthly active users who actively search for ideas and inspiration. 85% of Pinners use Pinterest to plan new projects. The platform has high commercial intent, with users 7x more likely to say Pinterest is the most influential platform in their purchase journey compared to other social media.

## PINTEREST USER DEMOGRAPHICS & BEHAVIOR
**Key Demographics:**
- 60% female, 40% male users
- Largest age groups: 25-34 (33%), 18-24 (23%), 35-44 (25%)
- Higher household income ($75K+): 45% of users
- 97% of searches are unbranded (discovery-focused)

**User Intent:**
- 89% use Pinterest for purchase inspiration
- 85% start projects based on Pinterest content
- 83% make purchases based on Pins from brands
- Average planning window: 2-3 months before purchase
- Categories: Home decor, fashion, beauty, food, DIY, wedding planning, travel

## PINTEREST CAMPAIGN STRUCTURE

### Campaign Hierarchy
**Campaign Level:**
- Campaign objective selection
- Campaign name and organization
- Campaign budget cap (optional)
- Campaign status

**Ad Group Level:**
- Targeting parameters (demographics, interests, keywords)
- Placement selection
- Budget and schedule
- Optimization goal and bid strategy
- Ad group name

**Pin/Ad Level:**
- Creative assets and Pin format
- Destination URL
- Pin title and description
- Call-to-action button

## PINTEREST CAMPAIGN OBJECTIVES

### Brand Awareness
- Maximize reach and impressions
- Best for: New brand introduction, seasonal campaigns, reaching new audiences
- Charged per: 1,000 impressions (CPM)
- Optimization: Impressions

### Video Views
- Maximize video plays
- Best for: Product demos, brand storytelling, tutorials
- Charged per: 1,000 impressions (CPM)
- Optimization: Video views (2-second minimum)

### Consideration
- Drive traffic to website or app
- Best for: Content promotion, blog traffic, product pages
- Charged per: Click (CPC)
- Optimization: Clicks, outbound clicks

### Conversions
- Drive specific actions on website (purchases, sign-ups, leads)
- Best for: E-commerce, lead generation, app installs
- Charged per: 1,000 impressions (CPM) or action
- Optimization: Conversions, value-based bidding available
- Requires: Pinterest Tag implementation

### Catalog Sales
- Promote products directly from product feed
- Best for: E-commerce, dynamic retargeting, product discovery
- Charged per: 1,000 impressions (CPM)
- Optimization: Product sales, ROAS

## PINTEREST AUDIENCE TARGETING

### Demographic Targeting
**Age:** 18-24, 25-34, 35-44, 45-49, 50-54, 55-64, 65+
**Gender:** All, Male, Female, Unspecified
**Location:** Countries, states/regions, metro areas (DMA), ZIP codes
**Language:** 30+ languages supported
**Device:** Mobile, desktop, tablet

### Interest Targeting
- 400+ interest categories across verticals
- Broad interests (e.g., Home Decor, Fashion, Beauty)
- Niche sub-categories (e.g., Modern Farmhouse Style, Sustainable Fashion)
- Automatically includes related interests
- Combine up to 25 interests per ad group

**Popular Categories:**
- DIY & Crafts, Food & Drink, Home Decor, Fashion, Beauty
- Health & Wellness, Travel, Parenting, Weddings, Gardening
- Technology, Finance, Business, Education, Vehicles

### Keyword Targeting
**Match Types:**
- Broad Match: Reaches related searches (automatic expansion)
- Phrase Match: Reaches searches containing exact phrase in any order
- Exact Match: Reaches only exact search terms
- Negative Keywords: Exclude irrelevant searches

**Keyword Strategy:**
- Use 25-75 keywords per ad group
- Include various match types for testing
- Leverage Pinterest Trends tool for seasonal keywords
- Monitor search terms report for optimization
- Target both aspirational and action-oriented keywords

**Keyword Categories:**
- Product keywords: "leather tote bag," "vintage coffee table"
- Instructional: "how to style curly hair," "easy dinner recipes"
- Project-based: "bathroom remodel ideas," "small backyard garden"
- Occasion-based: "Mother's Day gifts," "summer outfit ideas"

### Audience Targeting
**Actalike Audiences (Lookalikes):**
- Created from customer lists, website visitors, or engagement
- Expansion levels: 1-10% (1% most similar)
- Minimum source size: 100 people
- Best practices: Start with 1-2% for precision, 5-10% for scale

**Visitor Retargeting (Customer Lists):**
- Upload customer email/MAID lists
- Tag-based: website visitors (1-180 day window)
- Engagement audiences: people who engaged with your Pins
- Customer list audiences: existing customers, subscribers

**Engagement Retargeting:**
- People who engaged with Pins (saves, clicks, close-ups)
- Time windows: 1, 30, 60, 90, 180, 365 days
- Organic Pin engagers (not just paid ads)

**Expansion Options:**
- Enable expanded targeting for broader reach
- Algorithm finds similar audiences beyond defined parameters
- Recommended once narrow targeting is validated

## PINTEREST AD FORMATS

### Standard (Static) Pins
- Aspect ratio: 2:3 (1000 x 1500 pixels recommended)
- Maximum width: 1000 pixels
- File size: Up to 32MB
- Format: PNG or JPEG
- Best for: Product showcases, infographics, quotes, step-by-step guides
- Include text overlay for context (pins are often saved/shared without captions)

### Video Pins
- Aspect ratios: Square (1:1), Vertical (2:3, 9:16), Widescreen (16:9)
- Recommended: 2:3 for feed optimization
- Length: 4 seconds - 15 minutes (6-15 seconds recommended for ads)
- File size: Up to 2GB
- Format: .mp4, .mov, or .m4v
- First 3 seconds critical for engagement
- Auto-play with sound off (add captions)
- Best for: Tutorials, recipes, product demos, before/after

### Carousel Pins
- 2-5 images per Pin
- Each card can link to different URL
- Aspect ratio: 1:1 or 2:3
- Resolution: 1000 x 1000 or 1000 x 1500 pixels
- Best for: Product collections, storytelling, step-by-step processes, features showcase

### Shopping Ads
- Links directly to product pages
- Includes price, availability, product title
- Pulls from product catalog
- Best for: E-commerce, driving direct sales
- Features product details on Pin

### Collections
- Featured hero image/video + 3 secondary images below
- Mobile-only format
- Immersive shopping experience
- Hero asset: 1:1 ratio
- Secondary images: 1:1 ratio
- Best for: Curated product selections, themed collections

### Idea Pins (Organic, with boosting capability)
- Multi-page video/image format (up to 20 pages)
- Vertical format (9:16)
- Native creation tools (text, music, stickers)
- Cannot include external links (Pinterest-native)
- Can be boosted with ad spend
- Best for: Tutorials, storytelling, educational content, building following

## PINTEREST CREATIVE BEST PRACTICES

### Visual Design Principles
**Composition:**
- Vertical orientation performs best (2:3 aspect ratio ideal)
- Clean, uncluttered designs
- Strong focal point in top 2/3 of image
- Lifestyle imagery outperforms white background product shots
- Show products in use/context
- Bright, saturated colors perform better than muted tones
- Authentic, aspirational imagery (not overly polished)

**Text on Image:**
- Include descriptive text overlay
- Keep text concise (40 characters or fewer)
- Readable fonts (avoid script fonts)
- High contrast between text and background
- Position text in upper or middle third
- Avoid covering key visual elements

**Branding:**
- Include subtle logo/watermark
- Consistent brand colors and fonts
- Don't let branding dominate (soft integration)

### Copy Best Practices
**Pin Title:**
- 40-100 characters (100 max)
- Include main keyword
- Descriptive and specific
- Action-oriented when applicable

**Pin Description:**
- 200-500 characters (500 max)
- Expand on Pin content
- Include 3-5 relevant keywords naturally
- Add value (tips, benefits, details)
- Include hashtags (3-5 relevant ones)
- Write for both users and search algorithm

**Call-to-Action:**
- "Shop now," "Get the look," "Find your style," "Try this recipe"
- Align CTA with user intent and funnel stage
- Pinterest users respond well to inspirational, non-pushy CTAs

### Content Themes That Perform Well
- How-to guides and tutorials
- Before/after transformations
- Seasonal and holiday content (plan 45-60 days ahead)
- Curated lists ("10 ways to...," "Best ideas for...")
- Step-by-step processes
- Lifestyle contexts showing product use
- User-generated content and testimonials

## PINTEREST ADVERTISING BEST PRACTICES

### Strategic Timing
**Seasonal Planning:**
- Plan campaigns 45-90 days before peak season
- Pinterest users plan ahead (weddings, holidays, home projects)
- Major seasons: Back to School (June-Aug), Halloween (Aug-Oct), Holiday (Sept-Dec), Wedding (Jan-May)

**Day/Time Optimization:**
- Peak activity: Evenings (8-11 PM) and weekends
- Saturday is highest engagement day
- Consider "inspiration hours" (late night browsing)

### Bidding & Budgets
**Budget Recommendations:**
- Minimum daily budget: $1 per ad group
- Recommended: $5-10/day for testing
- $20+/day for consistent delivery and optimization
- Campaign learning phase: 50 conversions within 7 days

**Bid Strategy:**
- Start with automatic bidding
- Test max bid or target CPA after data collection
- Bid 10-30% higher for competitive keywords/interests
- Monitor average CPC and adjust accordingly

### Testing & Optimization
**Creative Testing:**
- Test 3-5 Pin variations per ad group
- Test different images, text overlays, descriptions
- Refresh creative every 30-45 days
- Seasonal creative updates essential

**Audience Testing:**
- Create separate ad groups for different targeting types
- Test interests vs. keywords
- Test broad vs. narrow targeting
- Expansion on/off tests

**Performance Monitoring:**
- Check performance every 2-3 days initially
- Weekly optimization after stabilization
- Watch for audience fatigue (declining CTR)
- Pause underperforming ads after 7 days of poor performance

### Pinterest Tag Implementation
- Install Pinterest Tag on all website pages
- Set up conversion events (PageVisit, AddToCart, Checkout, Purchase, SignUp, Lead)
- Enable Enhanced Match for better attribution
- Use event data for conversion optimization and retargeting
- Verify Tag installation with Pinterest Tag Helper browser extension

## PINTEREST PLATFORM ADVANTAGES
- High purchase intent (users actively planning purchases)
- Longer shelf life (Pins resurface in search over time)
- Lower competition and CPCs compared to Meta/Google
- Visual discovery environment (less ad fatigue)
- Affluent, engaged user base
- Strong performance in home, fashion, beauty, food, DIY verticals
