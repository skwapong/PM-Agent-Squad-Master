# KNOWLEDGE BASE 2: META (FACEBOOK & INSTAGRAM) ADVERTISING BEST PRACTICES

## PLATFORM OVERVIEW
Meta platforms (Facebook and Instagram) offer sophisticated advertising capabilities with 3+ billion combined users. Meta Ads Manager allows unified campaign management across both platforms with advanced targeting, creative formats, and optimization tools.

## META CAMPAIGN STRUCTURE

### 3-Tier Hierarchy
**Campaign Level:**
- Objective selection (e.g., Awareness, Traffic, Engagement, Leads, App Promotion, Sales)
- Campaign budget optimization (CBO) settings
- Campaign spending limits

**Ad Set Level:**
- Audience targeting and segmentation
- Placement selection (Facebook, Instagram, Audience Network, Messenger)
- Budget and schedule
- Optimization and delivery settings

**Ad Level:**
- Creative assets (images, videos, copy)
- Format selection
- Call-to-action buttons
- URL parameters and tracking

## META CAMPAIGN OBJECTIVES

### Awareness Objectives
**Brand Awareness:**
- Reach people more likely to recall ads
- Best for: New product launches, brand building
- Optimization: Ad recall lift, reach

**Reach:**
- Show ads to maximum number of people
- Best for: Local businesses, event promotion
- Optimization: Impressions, reach

### Consideration Objectives
**Traffic:**
- Drive visitors to website or app
- Best for: Content promotion, website traffic
- Optimization: Link clicks, landing page views

**Engagement:**
- Get more post engagement, page likes, event responses
- Best for: Community building, content engagement
- Optimization: Post engagement, page likes

**App Installs:**
- Drive mobile app downloads
- Best for: App launches, user acquisition
- Optimization: App installs, app events

**Video Views:**
- Increase video viewership
- Best for: Video content promotion, storytelling
- Optimization: ThruPlays, 2-second continuous views

**Lead Generation:**
- Collect leads via forms without leaving platform
- Best for: B2B, newsletters, quote requests
- Optimization: Leads, cost per lead

**Messages:**
- Start conversations in Messenger, Instagram, WhatsApp
- Best for: Customer service, personalized marketing
- Optimization: Messaging conversations

### Conversion Objectives
**Conversions:**
- Drive valuable actions on website, app, or Messenger
- Best for: E-commerce, sign-ups, purchases
- Optimization: Conversions, value, ROAS

**Catalog Sales:**
- Show products from catalog to drive sales
- Best for: E-commerce, dynamic retargeting
- Optimization: Conversions, catalog sales

**Store Traffic:**
- Drive visits to physical locations
- Best for: Retail, restaurants, multi-location businesses
- Optimization: Store visits, reach

## META AUDIENCE TARGETING

### Core Audiences
**Demographics:**
- Age (13-65+), Gender, Education, Job titles, Life events (recently engaged, new parents)

**Location Targeting:**
- Countries, states, cities, zip codes
- Radius targeting around addresses
- Inclusion/exclusion options
- People living in, recently in, or traveling to location

**Interests:**
- 1000+ interest categories
- Business & Industry, Entertainment, Family & Relationships, Fitness & Wellness, Food & Drink, Hobbies & Activities, Shopping & Fashion, Sports & Outdoors, Technology

**Behaviors:**
- Purchase behavior, device usage, travel patterns
- Digital activities, mobile device user, expats
- Anniversary within 30/61/90 days

**Connections:**
- People who like your page, attended your event
- Friends of people who like your page
- Exclude people who already like your page

### Custom Audiences
**Customer List:**
- Upload email addresses, phone numbers, user IDs
- Match rate typically 40-70%
- Use for customer retention, upselling

**Website Traffic:**
- Meta Pixel tracking required
- Target visitors based on pages visited, time on site, specific actions
- Retargeting windows: 1-180 days
- Best for warm audiences familiar with brand

**App Activity:**
- People who took actions in your app
- Requires Meta SDK implementation

**Engagement:**
- Video viewers (3s, 10s, 25%, 50%, 75%, 95%)
- Instagram profile visitors or engagers
- Form openers/submitters, ad clickers
- Facebook page engagers, event responders

**Offline Activity:**
- Upload in-store purchase data, phone call conversions
- Connect CRM data to ad platform

### Lookalike Audiences
- Create audiences similar to existing customers
- Based on Custom Audience source
- Similarity percentage: 1%-10% (1% most similar, 10% broadest)
- Best practices:
  - Use high-quality source audiences (1000+ people)
  - Start with 1-2% lookalikes for precision
  - Use 3-5% for scale
  - Create separate lookalikes for different customer segments
  - Exclude source audience to reach new people

### Advantage+ Audiences
- AI-powered audience expansion
- Provides audience suggestions as starting point
- Algorithm finds additional high-performing audiences
- Best for accounts with significant pixel data and conversion history

## META AD FORMATS

### Image Ads
- Recommended resolution: 1080 x 1080 pixels (square), 1200 x 628 (landscape)
- File type: JPG or PNG
- Text on image: Avoid >20% text overlay
- Best for: Simple messages, product showcases, promotions

### Video Ads
- Length: 15 seconds ideal, up to 241 minutes supported
- Recommended: 15-60 seconds for feed, 6-15 seconds for Stories
- Aspect ratios: 1:1 (square), 4:5 (vertical), 9:16 (Stories/Reels)
- Include captions (85% watch without sound)
- Hook within first 3 seconds
- Best for: Product demonstrations, storytelling, testimonials

### Carousel Ads
- 2-10 images or videos per ad
- Each card can have unique link
- Resolution: 1080 x 1080 recommended
- Great for: Product catalogs, features showcase, step-by-step guides
- Arrange cards by performance (algorithm auto-optimizes)

### Collection Ads
- Immersive mobile experience
- Cover image/video + product grid below
- Instant storefront experience
- Best for: E-commerce, showcasing multiple products
- Requires product catalog

### Stories Ads
- Full-screen vertical format (9:16)
- Resolution: 1080 x 1920 pixels
- 15-second maximum per Story
- Interactive elements: polls, questions, swipe-up links
- Best for: Limited-time offers, behind-the-scenes, urgent messaging

### Reels Ads
- Full-screen vertical video (9:16)
- Length: Up to 60 seconds (15-30 seconds recommended)
- Entertaining, authentic, trend-driven content performs best
- Best for: Reaching younger audiences, viral potential, entertainment value

### Instant Experience (Canvas)
- Full-screen mobile-only experience
- Combine images, videos, carousels, product catalogs
- Fast-loading, immersive
- Best for: Brand storytelling, product launches, engagement

## META ADVERTISING BEST PRACTICES

### Creative Excellence
- Test multiple creative variations (3-5 per ad set)
- Refresh creative every 2-4 weeks to prevent ad fatigue
- Use high-quality, authentic visuals
- Feature people using products when applicable
- Mobile-first design (90% of users on mobile)
- Strong visual hierarchy and clear focal point
- Brand logo visible but not dominant

### Copywriting Guidelines
- Headlines: 40 characters or fewer
- Primary text: 125 characters for optimal visibility
- Clear value proposition in first sentence
- Include specific benefits, not just features
- Use emotional triggers and power words
- Strong call-to-action (Shop Now, Learn More, Sign Up)
- Match ad copy tone to funnel stage
- Social proof when available (reviews, testimonials, user counts)

### Targeting Strategy
- Start with narrow, high-intent audiences
- Layer targeting criteria carefully (avoid over-narrowing)
- Create separate ad sets for different audiences
- Minimum audience size: 1,000 people (50,000+ ideal for conversions)
- Test broad targeting with Advantage+ for learning phase

### Budget & Bidding
- Minimum daily budget: $1 per ad set
- Learning phase: ~50 conversions per week per ad set
- Use Campaign Budget Optimization (CBO) for 3+ ad sets
- Start with automatic bidding, switch to manual after data collection
- Bid cap strategy for strict CPA goals
- Cost cap for balance of volume and efficiency

### Testing & Optimization
- Run A/B tests using Experiments feature
- Test one variable at a time
- Minimum test duration: 4-7 days
- Statistical significance required before conclusions
- Test elements: audiences, placements, creative, copy, offers
- Monitor frequency (ideal: 1-3, concerning: >5)

### Placement Optimization
- Start with Automatic Placements for best performance
- Review placement performance after 7 days
- Create separate campaigns for Stories/Reels vs Feed if creative differs significantly
- Consider Advantage+ Placements for AI optimization

## META PIXEL & CONVERSION TRACKING
- Install Meta Pixel on all website pages
- Set up standard events: ViewContent, AddToCart, InitiateCheckout, Purchase
- Use custom conversions for specific goals
- Enable Enhanced Measurement for better attribution
- Implement Conversions API for iOS 14+ tracking improvements
- Create audiences based on pixel events for retargeting
